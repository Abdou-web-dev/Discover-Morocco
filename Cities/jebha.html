Jebha

In northern Morocco, between Tangier and Nador, is the village of El Jebha. Rent a car and explore this small town, known for its tranquility and for its superb beaches.

On the Mediterranean coast of Morocco, the village of El Jebha is a little corner of paradise. Not much frequented by tourists, this port city is a haven of peace. People come there to rest and to enjoy the superb beaches in the area.

The beaches of El Jebha are indeed among the most beautiful and cleanest in the region. To the east of the city center, at the foot of the western Rif mountains, is the Marsedar cove, for example.

As if carved into the rock, this hidden beach has only a small strip of fine sand. With its turquoise water and raw nature, this cove cut off from the world is nevertheless among the most beautiful places in the country.

The port of El Jebha is also worth a visit. We walk there to admire the traditional boats and we stop in one of the small restaurants of the souk to taste fresh fish, in particular sardines - the specialty of the region.
