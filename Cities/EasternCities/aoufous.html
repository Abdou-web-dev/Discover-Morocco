Aoufous

A unique destination

In the Ziz valley, the inhabitants of Aoufous welcome you to help you discover the life of their Oasis. Far from the traditional circuits, closer to nature and the inhabitants, opt for a stay out of time and punctuated by unique encounters and discoveries.

The Aoufous valley offers stays based on the values ​​of Solidarity and Ethical Tourism of the AREMDT Network. Lovers of calm, nature and authenticity: the Aoufous valley will offer you a change of scenery and unique discoveries.

Solidarity tourism : It is in community that the men and women of Aoufous decided to open up to tourism. On the initiative of a small group wishing to welcome visitors to the valley, a local association was born to allow reflection on the social and solidarity values ​​to give to this project.

The definition of an oasis is "an area of ​​isolated vegetation in a desert. This occurs near a water source or when a water table is sufficiently close to the ground surface or sometimes on the river bed. coming to get lost in the desert. "

Located in the Saharan zone of Morocco, the Ziz valley is very representative of the oasis. Between two large desert plateaus, the valley with lush vegetation, crossed by the Oued Ziz (the Ziz river) which allows the irrigation of crops. In this area of ​​Morocco, the oases are home to farmers specializing in date palm.

Discovering oasis life means taking the time to get lost in the palm grove, this shaded area that allows a wide variety of fruits and vegetables to grow. You will walk between the plots, cross the water channels to arrive at the edge of the Ziz and enjoy its freshness.

You will meet there the men and women who work the land and who allow the oasis to live. The discovery of a traditional Ksar will take you back in history, to the time of the ancient battles between the Arabs and the Berbers, understand the architecture, designed to protect yourself from the heat of summer and the cold of the nights winter. The ocher earth, rock as far as the eye can see, impressive cliffs ... the landscapes of the Ziz valley offer the best that the desert has to offer: immensity.
