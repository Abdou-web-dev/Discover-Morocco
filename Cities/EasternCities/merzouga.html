Merzouga

Merzouga is a small Moroccan town located in the Sahara Desert, near the Algerian border. Merzouga opens onto Erg Chebbi, a huge expanse of sand dunes north of the city. To the west is Dayet Srji, a seasonal salt lake, often dry in summer. When it fills up again, it attracts a wide variety of migratory and desert birds, including warblers, nightjars and, occasionally, flamingos...

Merzouga is famous for its dunes , the highest in Morocco. The village adjoins the largest erg in Morocco, erg Chebbi.
Merzouga has also been known by students from France and Europe, for more than a decade, to be the base for the famous student Raid, the Raid 4L Trophy, which sets up its bivouac there with its 1,200 cars every year.

Merzouga has become a major tourist attraction for this region of Morocco, allowing the development of a hotel industry, both locally and in the neighboring towns of Errachidia and Erfoud .

The activities offered to tourists are camel trekking, bivouac in the desert, quad biking, and, in summer, arenotherapy (or sand bath) also called psammatotherapy.

The balance of the Merzouga site is threatened by the anarchic construction of hotels, the overcrowding of tourists and the development of "mechanical sports" (quad).
This hotel tourism has an ecological impact: overexploitation of water resources and non-compliance with wastewater treatment rules.

The sand Dunes

The Dunes of Merzouga or "Erg Chebbi", located about 40 km from Algeria, are considered to be the doors of the desert. They represent the only Saharan erg, with the Chigaga erg, which is located on Moroccan territory. These dunes cover a large plot of about twenty kilometers in length and can reach up to 150 meters in height. A word of advice, don't miss the sunset or the sunrise from the dunes for anything. It is simply unforgettable. Embellish all this with a little camel ride to make your outing much more fun and enjoyable. Be careful however in the sun, temperatures can reach between 45 and 50 ° in the sun.

Erg Chebbi Desert

Riding a camel surrounded by km of fine sand, looking at the high dunes of the desert around you, sleeping in a tent or outside looking at the stars, dreaming in the silence of the night wondering what each is. little light you see in the sky, waking up with the sun on the top of a dune ...... This is all we can do in the Erg Chibbi desert. Located in the south-east of Morocco, on the border with Algeria, at the foot of Merzouga, a few km from Rissani and 40km from Erfoud, well connected by a paved road near the desert itself .
